Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using Rakshita

How do I trigger an SOS alert?

Simply press the volume button 5 times quickly on your phone. The app will automatically detect this gesture and send an SOS alert to your emergency contacts.

How do I add emergency contacts?

Go to the app settings, select "Emergency Contacts", and tap the "+" button to add new contacts. You can add multiple trusted contacts who will receive your alerts.

What happens when I trigger an SOS?

When you trigger an SOS, the app immediately shares your precise location with all your emergency contacts via SMS and push notifications. They can then take appropriate action.

Is my location data secure?

Yes, all location data is encrypted and only shared with your designated emergency contacts. We never store or share your location data with third parties.

Can I customize the SOS gesture?

Currently, the SOS gesture is fixed to 5 volume button presses for reliability and quick access. We're working on customizable gestures for future updates.

What if I accidentally trigger an SOS?

You can cancel an SOS alert within 10 seconds of triggering it. Simply tap the "Cancel" button that appears on your screen, and no alert will be sent.
